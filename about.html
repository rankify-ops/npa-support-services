About NPA Support Services

High-quality, practical and person-centred support.

NPA Support Services was created to provide high-quality, practical and person-centred support to people with disability and their families.

Make a Referral Contact Us
Why NPA exists

Everyone deserves a fresh start.

A main focus of NPA Support Services is supporting participants to have a fresh start. We believe people should be supported to move forward from historical behaviours, incidents and labels.

“A person’s history should be understood — but it should not always define who they are, what they are capable of, or the opportunities available to them.”

Warm & collaborative

We believe families and support teams need strategies that are clear, realistic and easy to implement in everyday life.

Strengths-based

Focused on opportunity, safety, skill building and future growth — not defining people by behaviours of concern.

Evidence-based & ethical

Grounded in person-centred principles, advocacy and improving quality of life.

Meet Nadia

Experienced, evidence-based and person-centred.

Advanced Level Positive Behaviour Support Practitioner · Experienced Supervisor & Mentor

Nadia is an Advanced Level Positive Behaviour Support Practitioner and experienced supervisor with a background in primary education. She brings a strong foundation in behaviour analysis, teaching, developmental support, staff training and capacity building.

Nadia has experience supporting children, young people and adults with complex disability support needs. She has worked with participants, families, schools, SIL providers, day programs, support workers and allied health teams to understand behaviour, reduce risk, improve consistency and support safer daily routines.

Her practice is grounded in evidence-based and person-centred principles, with a strong focus on advocacy, improving quality of life, reducing restrictive practices, recognising participant strengths and building the confidence of the people around the participant.

Experience & strengths

What Nadia brings to every participant

Clinical experience

  • Functional Behaviour Assessments to identify the root causes and purpose of behaviours of concern
  • Interim and comprehensive Behaviour Support Plans for diverse and complex needs
  • Reviewing PBS reports for clinical quality, ethical practice and NDIS compliance
  • Supporting complex behaviours, transitions and high-support needs
  • Commitment to reducing restrictive practices and improving quality of life

Teaching & capacity building

  • One-on-one coaching and training for staff, families and key stakeholders
  • Supervising and mentoring PBS practitioners
  • Translating complex behavioural concepts into clear, practical, achievable strategies
  • Collaborating with multidisciplinary teams — families, educators, allied health, SIL providers and day programs
  • Bachelor of Education (Primary), Diploma of Children’s Services, current Graduate Diploma of Psychology study

Privacy, Complaints & Safeguarding

Your privacy, rights and safety matter.

NPA Support Services respects participant privacy and confidentiality, welcomes all feedback and complaints, and is committed to safe, ethical and respectful support.

Contact Us Provide Feedback
Our commitments

How we protect you

Privacy & confidentiality

NPA Support Services respects participant privacy and confidentiality. We only collect information that is needed to provide safe and appropriate supports.

Consent & information sharing

Information will only be shared with consent, unless there is a legal, safeguarding or safety requirement to share information.

Feedback & complaints welcome

Participants, families, carers and providers are encouraged to provide feedback or raise concerns. Complaints will be taken seriously and responded to respectfully.

Safeguarding & incident management

NPA Support Services is committed to safe, ethical and respectful support. Any risks, incidents or safeguarding concerns will be responded to promptly and in line with duty of care, privacy obligations and participant rights.

Urgent safety concerns should be directed to emergency services on 000. NPA Support Services is not an emergency or crisis response service.

Feedback, compliments & complaints

What happens when you raise a concern

NPA Support Services welcomes all feedback, compliments and complaints. Feedback helps us understand what we are doing well and where we can improve our services.

1

Lodge it your way

Directly with a staff member (verbally or via the feedback form), by email to nadia@npasupportservices.com.au, or in writing to Director, NPA Support Services, PO Box 15, Rosanna VIC 3084.

2

Acknowledged within 2 working days

Your complaint will be formally acknowledged within two working days of being received.

3

Responded to within 28 days

We aim to respond to all complaints and grievances as quickly as possible, and within 28 days from acknowledgement wherever possible.

Treated seriously, respectfully and confidentially. NPA Support Services will use feedback and complaints to continuously improve the quality and safety of our service delivery.

If you are not satisfied with the outcome

External agencies you can contact

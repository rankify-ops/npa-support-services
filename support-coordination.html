Support Coordination

Helping you understand and use your NDIS plan.

Support Coordination helps participants and families understand their NDIS plan, connect with services and build the skills and confidence to coordinate supports.

Request Support Coordination Make a Referral
How we help

Your plan, your choices — with support along the way.

NPA Support Services works alongside participants, families, nominees and providers to support clear communication, service connection and practical plan implementation. We value participant choice and control — our role is to help people understand their options and make informed decisions about the supports that best meet their needs.

Understanding your NDIS plan

Your plan and funded supports explained in clear, plain language.

Connecting with providers

Helping participants and families connect with suitable providers.

Coordinating your supports

Coordinating therapists, providers, support teams and services so they work together.

Choice and control

Supporting participants to understand their options and make informed decisions.

Service agreements & set-up

Assistance with service agreements and getting supports up and running.

Communication between services

Supporting clear communication so everyone stays on the same page.

Plan reassessment preparation

Getting ready for plan reassessments and reviews with the right information.

Resolving service issues

Helping resolve service issues and gaps in support.

Building your capacity

Building participant and family skills and confidence to manage supports over time.

Need a higher level of coordination? For participants with more complex needs, risks or multiple service systems involved, we also provide Specialist Support Coordination.

Next steps

Ready to get more from your NDIS plan?

Request Support Coordination and we’ll be in touch to talk through your plan, your goals and how we can help.

Specialist Support Coordination

A higher level of coordination for complex needs.

Specialist Support Coordination provides a higher level of coordination for participants with more complex needs, risks or service systems involved.

Request Specialist Support Coordination Contact Us
How we help

Clarity and stability when things are complex.

NPA Support Services can assist participants, families and care teams to identify service gaps, coordinate providers, clarify next steps and support safer, more consistent implementation of the NDIS plan — including during major transitions, provider changes, school or day program concerns, safeguarding concerns or complex family and service coordination needs.

Complex support needs

Support for participants with complex needs, high-risk situations, service breakdowns or multiple systems involved.

Multi-system coordination

Complex family, housing, health, school, SIL, justice, child protection or safeguarding needs, where relevant.

Coordinating multiple providers

Bringing providers together and helping clarify roles and responsibilities.

Transitions & service breakdowns

Support during major transitions, changes in living arrangements or provider changes.

Identifying gaps & risks

Helping identify service gaps, risks and practical next steps.

Care team communication

Supporting communication across care teams and stakeholders.

Complex plan reassessments

Preparation and evidence gathering for complex plan reassessments.

Safer, sustainable supports

Supporting the participant and their network toward safer, more stable and sustainable supports.

NPA Support Services is not an emergency or crisis response service. If there is an immediate risk of harm, please contact emergency services on 000.

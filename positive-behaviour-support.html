Positive Behaviour Support

Understanding the person behind the behaviour.

Positive Behaviour Support helps to understand why behaviours of concern are happening and what support a person needs to feel safe, understood and included.

Make a PBS Referral Contact Us
What is PBS?

Behaviour is often a form of communication.

At NPA Support Services, we focus on understanding the person behind the behaviour. Our role is to identify what the person may be trying to communicate, what is causing distress, and what practical supports can reduce risk and improve quality of life.

We work with families, carers, support workers, schools, SIL providers, day programs and allied health teams to create strategies that are respectful, realistic and consistent.

Feel safe & understood

Supports built around what the person is telling us.

Build skills & reduce risk

Practical strategies for safer, more consistent daily routines.

Work toward fewer restrictive practices

Safer, less restrictive supports wherever possible.

What NPA can provide

Positive Behaviour Support services

From assessment through to practical, everyday implementation — support for children, young people and adults.

Functional Behaviour Assessments

Understanding the root causes, context and purpose of behaviours of concern.

Interim Behaviour Support Plans

Timely plans that put practical, positive strategies in place early.

Comprehensive Behaviour Support Plans

Detailed, evidence-based plans for participants with diverse and complex needs.

Behaviour intervention planning

Positive strategies focused on skill development, independence and safer daily routines.

Restrictive practice review & reduction

Reviewing current practices and planning to work toward safer, less restrictive supports.

Staff & family training and coaching

One-on-one coaching, training and implementation support for the people around the participant.

Data collection & behaviour tracking

Practical tools to track behaviour and review progress over time.

Data-informed adjustments

Refining strategies and implementation recommendations as we learn what works.

Visual supports & practical resources

Routines, scripts, visual supports and resources that make strategies easy to use.

Transition support

Support through school, day program, home or SIL changes.

Children, young people & adults

Support for people with behaviours of concern across ages and stages of life.

Working with your whole team

Families, carers, support workers, schools, SIL providers, day programs and allied health.

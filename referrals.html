Referrals · Melbourne & Victoria

Make a referral to NPA Support Services.

Refer for Positive Behaviour Support, Support Coordination or Specialist Support Coordination — or a combination. Please provide as much information as possible so we can review suitability and get back to you.

1. Submit the form

Choose the referral type below and complete the secure form with participant details and any relevant background — it goes straight into our practice system.

2. We review it

NPA Support Services will review the information provided to consider suitability, availability and the best way to support the participant.

3. We’re in touch

We’ll contact you to discuss next steps. If NPA isn’t the right fit, we’ll do our best to point you in a helpful direction.

Referral form

Start your referral

Choose the service you’re referring for, then complete the secure form below. Referrals go straight into our practice system and waitlist, so nothing gets missed.

Referring for Specialist Support Coordination? Use the Support Coordination form and mention Specialist Support Coordination in your referral.

By submitting a referral you confirm you have the consent or authority of the participant (or their representative) to provide this information to NPA Support Services. NPA Support Services is not an emergency or crisis response service — if there is an immediate risk of harm, please contact emergency services on 000.

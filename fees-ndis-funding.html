Fees & NDIS Funding

Clear and fair, in line with NDIS pricing.

Services are billed in line with the current NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits where applicable — and you’ll always have a clear service agreement before services commence.

Contact Us Make a Referral
How it works

Simple, transparent billing

1

NDIS pricing applies

Services are billed in line with the current NDIS Pricing Arrangements and Price Limits where applicable. You can view the current pricing on the official NDIS pricing page.

2

Service agreement first

Before services commence, NPA Support Services will provide a service agreement outlining the supports to be delivered, how services will be provided, costs, payment arrangements and how changes can be made.

3

Supports begin

Once you’re comfortable with the agreement, supports begin — with clear communication about how your funding is being used along the way.

Where funding comes from

Which part of the NDIS plan funds each service?

Positive Behaviour Support

Usually funded under Improved Relationships (Capacity Building) in the NDIS plan.

Support Coordination

Funded under the relevant Support Coordination funding in the NDIS plan.

Specialist Support Coordination

Funded under the relevant Support Coordination funding for higher-level coordination needs.

Self-managed, plan-managed or agency-managed? Participants can be self-managed, plan-managed or agency-managed, where applicable to service registration status. If you’re unsure whether your NDIS plan includes funding for these supports, contact us and we can help review this with you.

Questions about funding?

Not sure what your plan covers?

Get in touch and we’ll help you work out whether your NDIS plan includes funding for Positive Behaviour Support, Support Coordination or Specialist Support Coordination.

Work With Us

Interested in working with us?

NPA Support Services is open to connecting with passionate practitioners, support coordinators, specialist support coordinators, contractors, students and administration staff who share our values.

Submit an Expression of Interest Contact Us
Who we look for

People who share our values

We value people who are warm, reliable, professional, reflective and committed to improving quality of life for people with disability. Whether you’re an experienced practitioner or just starting out, we’d love to hear from you.

PBS Practitioners

Deliver person-centred behaviour support with supervision and mentoring available.

Support Coordinators

Help participants and families understand and implement their NDIS plans.

Specialist Support Coordinators

Work with participants with complex needs, risks and multiple services.

Admin & intake support

Keep referrals, scheduling and communication running smoothly.

Students & placements

Learn within an ethical, values-led practice.

Contractors & subcontractors

Flexible arrangements for experienced practitioners with an ABN.

Report writing support

Support high-quality, compliant clinical documentation.

Supervision & mentoring

Professional supervision for PBS practitioners and students may be available.
